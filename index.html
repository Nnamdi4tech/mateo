How To Prepare
Green Coffee

  • Grind the green coffee beans, then take one glass of water 150ml or 1 sachet water. Put one table spoon of green coffee beans in the water and mix.
  • Put the mix content in a stove/gas or any heating means and bring to boil for about 8-10mins over low heat approx.
  • Sieve the health green coffee beverage and drink! For better taste add honey or cardamon only
  • Dosage: Drink 30mins before breafast, and 3omins before dinner.
